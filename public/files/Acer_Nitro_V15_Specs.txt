**Acer Nitro V15 Laptop Specification**

**General**
- Model: Acer Nitro V15
- Series: Nitro
- Target Audience: Gaming & High‑Performance Users

**Processor**
- Intel® Core™ i5‑1135G7 (10‑core, up to 4.2 GHz)
- Intel® Core™ i7‑1165G7 (10‑core, up to 4.7 GHz)
- 2.8 GHz Base Clock
- 16 GB DDR4 LP‑DDR4X (up to 4267 MHz)

**Graphics**
- Integrated: Intel® Iris® Xe Graphics
- Dedicated: NVIDIA GeForce® RTX 3050 Ti (4 GB GDDR6, 2×2 GB, or 6 GB GDDR6 options)

**Storage**
- 512 GB NVMe SSD (PCIe Gen 3)
- Optional 1 TB NVMe SSD or 1 TB HDD

**Display**
- 15.6” Full HD (1920×1080) IPS panel
- 144 Hz refresh rate (gaming mode), 60 Hz standard
- 300 cd/m² brightness, 250 % contrast ratio

**Keyboard & Touchpad**
- Back‑lit keyboard (RGB, 4‑zone)
- Multi‑touch Precision touchpad (Microsoft compatible)

**Audio**
- 2 W stereo speakers with Acer‑Acoustics 360° audio
- Headphone/mic combo jack

**Connectivity**
- Wi‑Fi 6 (802.11ax) + Bluetooth 5.1
- Ports:
  * 2× USB‑3.1 Gen 1 Type‑A
  * 1× USB‑3.1 Gen 2 Type‑C (supporting USB‑Power Delivery, DisplayPort 1.4, and Thunderbolt 4)
  * 1× HDMI 2.0b
  * 1× Mini‑DisplayPort 1.4
  * 1× RJ‑45 Ethernet (10/100/1000 Mbps)
  * 1× 3.5 mm headphone/microphone combo jack
  * 1× SD card reader

**Battery & Power**
- 4‑Cell 56 Wh Li‑Polymer battery
- Up to 10 hrs battery life (typical 8 hrs under moderate use)
- AC adapter: 220‑240 V/50‑60 Hz, 90 W

**Dimensions & Weight**
- Height: 2.2 cm
- Width: 35.5 cm
- Depth: 24.4 cm
- Weight: 2.3 kg (5.1 lb)

**Operating System**
- Windows 11 Home (64‑bit) pre‑installed (upgrade available to Pro)

**Warranty**
- 1‑year limited warranty (hardware, parts, and labor)

**Notes**
- Optional NVIDIA GeForce RTX 3050 Ti upgrade for enhanced gaming performance.
- Gaming mode optimises fan speed and performance.
- The model supports 2‑in‑1 touch functionality on the display.

*This specification list is based on the typical configuration released in 2022 and may vary slightly by region or retailer.*
